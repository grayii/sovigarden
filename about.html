About Us

We are more than a plant shop. We bring life, beauty, and nature into your home and workspace with curated greenery.

Our Mission

To curate a collection of high-quality indoor plants that are easy to care for, resilient, and perfect for both beginners and seasoned plant enthusiasts. We aim to bring a touch of nature to every corner of the world.

Our Vision

Sustainability is at the heart of our journey. From eco-friendly packaging to minimizing our carbon footprint, we strive to create a greener, healthier planet for future generations

Our Story

At SOVI, we started with a simple goal: to make indoor greenery accessible to everyone. From humble beginnings, we have grown into a community of plant lovers who value sustainability, creativity, and beauty. Join us in cultivating a greener tomorrow.
